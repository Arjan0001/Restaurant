Our Story

A Legacy of Royal Flavours

Nestled in the heart of Pune, The Royal Spice has been the city's most cherished address for connoisseurs of fine dining. We blend the richness of authentic Indian spice traditions with the refinement of continental technique, served within an ambience of understated luxury. Every plate tells a story of heritage, craftsmanship and passion.
